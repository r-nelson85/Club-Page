Who Are We?

Sons of Gwanganli was founded in 2017, by a group of missfits and reprobates living in Busan, South Korea. For a bit of fun, we created this club embracing the spirit of motorcycle culture and freedom. As a club, and as the people within it, we evolved from cheap shitty motorcyles made in Korea, to Japanese beauties from such brands as Honda, Yamaha, and Suzuki.

We began as outlaws without licenses and documentation, times changed, and we had no choice but to follow suit. Still, the spirit of our club remains - The love of two wheels and adventure, and the freedoms that it affords us.

Events and Activities

Day Trips

Once or twice a month we head out of the city in search of the awesome mountain twisties and beautiful coast roads that Korea has to offer.

Camping

We go on multiple camping trips throughout the year. This enables us to go further afield and visit even more amazing places around the country.

Bike Nights

Every second Wednesday of the month we have our bike night meetups. Usually involving a group ride to a coffee shop and some excellent banter.

Poker Runs

Once a year we hold our annual poker run. Draw a card at each stop, best hand wins at the end of the day!
